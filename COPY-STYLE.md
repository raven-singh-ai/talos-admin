# ✍️ TalosPro Copy Style — Quick Reference

## Product Name
✅ TalosPro  
❌ Talos Pro, talospro

## Authentication
✅ Sign in / Sign out  
❌ Login / Log out

## Buttons
✅ Title Case for primary CTAs: "Sign In", "Get Started"  
✅ Sentence case for secondary: "View all", "Cancel"

## Error Messages
✅ Specific + Actionable: "Incorrect email or password"  
❌ Generic: "Error", "Failed"

## Empty States
✅ Format: "[What's missing] — [Context]"  
✅ Example: "No users yet — they'll appear here when someone signs up"

## Loading States
✅ Personal + Specific: "Loading your dashboard..."  
❌ Generic: "Loading..."

## Search
✅ Tell what's searchable: "Search by name, email, or phone"  
❌ Generic: "Search..."
